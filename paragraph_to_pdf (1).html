Paragraph → PDF

Paste your paragraph on the left, tweak font-size and line-spacing on the right, click Generate PDF. No server — all magic happens in your browser.

Note: For faithful on-screen typography we use the Roboto web font. The PDF uses the browser-side jsPDF standard font (Helvetica) which ensures excellent portability. If you want embedded Roboto in the PDF you must supply the Roboto TTF as base64 — see comments in the HTML source.
